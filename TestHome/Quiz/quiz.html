How to Play

  • You have 10 Questions to answer
  • Each question is multiple choice
  • Take too long to answer and you start losing potential points
  • If you're stuck, use the hint button
  • Using the hint button will also cost you points
  • Select Begin! to Begin!
  • Good Luck!

How to Enter

  • Once you have completed the quiz, you'll be presented with your score
  • Take a screenshot as evidence
  • Skype a screenshot to the Quizmaster
  • This an honour system, so no cheating by taking lots of turns!
  • If you have any problems, come find/ skype Alyn!
